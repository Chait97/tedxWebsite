About

WHAT IS TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxIITHyderabad, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxIITHyderabad event, TEDTalks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. The TED Conference provides general guidance for the TEDx program, but individual TEDx events, including ours, are self-organized.


WHY IS IIT-HYDERABAD ORGANISING TEDxIITHyderabad?

Indian Institute of Technology, Hyderabad, one of the premier educational institutions in India is hosting the third edition of TEDxIITHyderabad. Though we are young, we have a great vision, agility, commitment and energy to create an innovative research environment.
At TEDxIITHyderabad, we believe it is important to invest in areas where the local community finds its relevance. Consequently, TEDxIITHyderabad is an effort to bring together and showcase the bright minds from the local scene- we are continuously in awe of how much Hyderabad has to offer in terms of the quality of ideas and number of people ready to share their stories with the world.


LAUNCHING TEDxIITHYDERABAD 2017

At IIT- Hyderabad, believe in transforming ideas into reality. And here we are taking a significant step towards this mission – TEDxIITHyderabad 2017. TEDxIITHyderabad is an annual programme aimed at educating, inspiring and connecting great minds. TEDxIITHyderabad 2017, a journey in itself, began with the search for a theme. The theme for this edition is “Tomorrow Starts Now”. The event will be a day long feature where the speakers go live along with presentations of TED Talks Videos.

Speakers

KK Senthil Kumar

KK Senthil Kumar is the award winning cinemtatographer behind movies like Bahubali, Magadheera, Eega and Arundhati. Known for his grand setups and large scale action sequences, He received SIIMA award for Best Cinematographer for Eega. Senthil has blurred the line berween VFX and Reality in his movies and has received critical acclaim for his work.

Professor B.M. Hegde is a medical scientist, educationist and author who has contributed immensely in the field of healthcare medical science, and is known for his holistic approach. He has been bestowed the Dr B.C. Roy National Award in the category of an Eminent Medical Teacher in 1999 and the Padma Bhushan award in 2010. He has also served as the Vice-Chancellor of Manipal University. He is currently working on putting together a system of medical care delivery which is authentic, inexpensive, patient friendly and easily accessible.

Prof B.M. Hegde

Arpan Shah

Arpan Shah is a technologist, investor and data scientist in Silicon Valley. A graduate of Stanford University, Arpan specializes in Systems and Data. He is currently head of data at Robinhood, one of the most successful Fintech companies in the Bay Area, and also advises and invests in startups with his investment firm in India.

The inventor of revolutionary 'skybus metro’, Mr. Rajaram has worked as the MD and CEO of the Konkan Railway corporation. Graduating from Andhra University, Rajaram pursued his masters from IIT Kharagpur. He worked on field operations of Indian railways, and research and development program in railway technologies , and was a consultant to many countries. He has worked on many projects like Anti Collision Device, Moving Block System, Self Stabilising Track, etc., improving the reliability of the railways at a low cost.

Rajaram Bojji

Aditi Avasthi

Aditi Avasthi is the Founder and CEO at Embibe.com. Embibe is an exciting artificial intelligence platform that is personalising the delivery of education to students in emerging markets. It's a complete platform with a deep stack on content intelligence using computer vision; student intelligence built using custom neural networks, culminating in mentor intelligence which leverages the other two

Anam Hashim is a professional bike stunt performer and rider, and India's only female streetbike freestyle athlete. She also runs an organisation to promote Stunt Riding and Adventure Sports in India. She is breaking stereotypes in a male-dominated profession and is a youth icon in her own right.

Anam Hashim

Richa Singh

Richa Singh is the Co-founder of yourdost.com. Graduating from IIT Guwahati, she began her company YourDost, an emotional support system for people to discuss problems with qualified and experienced individuals, in anonymity. She is a young entrepreneur wanting to change the stigma around seeking emotional well-being in the country.

Bari Anwar, better known as "ZeDDisbug", is one of the first Indian e-sportsmen. Soon after college, he went on to captain Beyond Infinity - undoubtedly the best Indian Dota 2 team, until it underwent a merger and came to be called Entity Esports. Becoming a professional gamer in India was not easy, however. He has been swimming against the current throughout his life to get to where he is now.

Bari Anwar
